Application to leave the NHS Pension Scheme

Use this service to:

  • opt out of the NHS Pension Scheme
  • stop making pension contributions

This form was previously called the SD502 form.

Opting out takes about 10 minutes

Before you start

There are two parts to this form. The first is for completion by the employee. The second is for completion by the authority (the employer).

If you are an employee, you will need:

  • a contact email for the Payroll Department at your employer.
  • your payroll number, you will find this on a payslip or your employer will be able to tell you this.

You can also opt out by post.

This service is also available in Welsh (Cymraeg).
